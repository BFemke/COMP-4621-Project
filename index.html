About Us

At TimberTech Logging, we are dedicated to delivering top-notch logging services that are built on a foundation of integrity, excellence, and environmental stewardship. With a commitment to sustainable forestry practices and a team of highly skilled professionals, we take pride in our role as a leading logging contractor company in the industry.

Our History

Established in 1995, TimberTech Logging has a rich history that spans over two decades. Our journey began with a small team of loggers who shared a passion for the timber industry. Over the years, we've grown into a respected contractor, known for our dedication to the responsible management of forests.

Our Mission

Mission: To provide sustainable logging services that meet the highest industry standards while minimizing our environmental impact.
At TimberTech Logging, our mission is clear: we strive to meet the timber needs of our clients while ensuring the long-term health of the forests we work in. We are committed to preserving the environment and ecosystems for future generations.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our business operations and relationships.
  • Safety: We prioritize the safety of our employees, clients, and the environment in every project we undertake.
  • Quality: We deliver top-quality timber products while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to sustainable logging practices that minimize waste and protect our natural resources.
